Oakheart


Oakheart follows the story of Melduro, a young man setting out from his family home to go and learn at the Crowned Hill. Having reached the age of 16 Melduro's father, the Feldan of Delwood Hill, agrees to send his son to the Crowned Hill where he will be educated and initiated by the mysterious order of Druids. Join Melduro as he makes friends, falls in love, and uncovers shadowy plots he may not live to regret.

Time of Decay


As Melduro recovers from his injuries at the hands of the Cult of the Decay, the captured cultists are escorted north to be put on trial for their crimes. For a time everything seems well but something festers below the surface. Rejoin Melduro as he contiues his educations under the druids and somehow finds himself at the center of another plot. Can he unravel the threads of secrecy before it's too late?
